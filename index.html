About Us

HealthAI is an AI-powered solution for medical professionals and patients that allows them to use our powerful AI tool to generate risk-reports and calculate the risk of illness based on lifestyle and prior medical history. Anywhere you are in the world you can use our website to connect to your patients and help them with their problems. With just one click of a button you can generate a report showing the probability of them being afflicted with heart disease lung cancer or asthma.
